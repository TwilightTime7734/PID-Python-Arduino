For the Betafpv Pavo Pico 2 start with
Roll:  P 40, D 17, I 30, FF 0
Pitch: P 42, D 17, I 35, FF 0
Yaw:   leave low/not tested for now

Now sweep upward:
Roll P: 40, 42, 45, 48
Pitch P: 42, 44, 47, 50

```text
Start with P, D, and a conservative baseline I
Set Roll I = 30 and Pitch I = 35
Set FF = 0

Start:
Roll:  P 45, D 17, I 30
Pitch: P 47, D 17, I 35
Yaw:   P 45, D low/0

Keep battery fresh.

Arm once for all logs if possible.

D tuning:
Log with D 17
Log with D 23
Log with D 30
Log with D 36
Optional D 42 only if needed and motors stay cool.

Open PIDToolbox.
Trim each log.
Use Step Response Tool.
Always hit Y Correction.
Hit Run.
Pick the D value with best damping and least overshoot.

P tuning:
Keep chosen D.
Log with lower P.
Log with current P.
Log with higher P.
Log with still higher P if safe.

Example:
Roll P: 40, 45, 50, 55
Pitch P: 42, 47, 52, 57
Yaw P: 40, 45, 50, 55

Use Step Response Tool.
Always hit Y Correction.
Pick the highest P that tracks well without ringing or oscillation.

Re-check D:
With chosen P, test D slightly lower/current/higher.
Pick best P/D combination.

I tuning:
With correct P & D set:
           R    P    Y
log with I:35,  40,  35
log with I:60,  65,  60
log with I:85,  90,  85
log with I:110,115, 110

Use Step Response Tool, but also inspect main trace.
Pick the value that holds attitude without slow wobble or bounce-back.

FF tuning:
With correct P, D, and I set:
           R      P      Y
log with FF:43,   44,   43
log with FF:86,   89,   86
log with FF:129, 134,  129
log with FF:172, 179,  172

Use main PIDToolbox graph.
Click axis graph.
Use zoom magnifying glass.
Pick FF where gyro starts with setpoint without jumping ahead.
```
